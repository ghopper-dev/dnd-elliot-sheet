Cantrips (at-will)

Thaumaturgy Transmutation · 1 action · 30 ft. · V

Minor wonder for 1 minute (up to 3 active, dismiss as an action): your voice booms ×3; flames flicker/brighten/dim/change color; harmless tremor; an instantaneous sound (thunder, raven's cry, ominous whispers) from a point in range; an unlocked door/window slams open or shut; your eyes change appearance.

1st Level · 4 slots

Hunter's Mark Divination · 1 bonus action · 90 ft. · V · Concentration, up to 1 hour

Mark a creature. Your weapon attacks vs it deal +1d6 Force damage, and you have advantage on checks to find/track it. If it drops to 0 HP before the spell ends, you can move the mark to a new creature with a later bonus action. (2024: damage is now Force; Rangers get it for free & it's iconic.)

Cure Wounds Abjuration · 1 action · Touch · V,S

A creature regains 2d8 + Wis mod HP. Upcast: +2d8 per slot above 1st.

Speak with Animals Divination · 1 action (or Ritual) · Self · V,S · 10 min

Talk to Beasts. They can tell you what they've seen nearby. Persuasion checks with Beasts may be influenced at DM's discretion.

2nd Level · 3 slots

Pass without Trace Abjuration · 1 action · Self · V,S · Concentration, up to 1 hour

Veil yourself + companions within 30 ft.: +10 to Dexterity (Stealth) checks, leave no tracks, and can't be tracked except by magical means. (2024: aura still 30 ft.) — pairs beautifully with Perfect Hide.

3rd Level · 2 slots

Nondetection Abjuration · 1 action · Touch · V,S,M (25 gp diamond dust, consumed) · 8 hours

Target can't be targeted by Divination magic or perceived through magical scrying sensors. No concentration.

⚠️ 2024 Ranger is a prepared caster — you can swap spells on a long rest. Slots: 4 / 3 / 2. Casting stat: Wisdom.
